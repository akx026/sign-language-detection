About Our Sign Language Detection System

Our project aims to bridge the communication gap between individuals who use sign language and those who do not. By leveraging AI and computer vision, this system translates sign language gestures into readable text in real-time.

Unlike traditional methods, which require expensive hardware or human interpreters, our system provides an accessible and cost-effective solution. Users can interact using their webcam without the need for additional devices.

This initiative promotes inclusivity, enabling individuals with speech and hearing impairments to communicate more effectively in daily life, education, and workplaces.
